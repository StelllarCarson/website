Fossil Fighters (Nintendo DS 2009)

I still remember stepping off the boat as a rookie on Vivosaur Island ready to become a fossil fighter. They handed me a pickaxe, sonar, and a fossil bag to find fossils, then I clean 'em and bring 'em back to life. Then came the real fun: climbing the 3v3 tournament ranks and scouting for more dinos and challengers. A solid 10/10 gem in my memory.

Dragon Quest Monsters: Joker 1

A hidden RPG classic—its soundtrack was off the charts catchy (as usual for the series). It was a good monster scouting game.

Di-Gata Defenders

An artful platformer that used the DS’s dual screens in a way no one else did.

Final Fantasy Tactics A2

This strategy gem came with maps galore—perfect for long commutes and epic battles.

Pokémon Mystery Dungeon: Explorers of Time

A heartfelt narrative experience that rivals full-blown console adventures.
